About

After a rewarding decade in the retail industry, I realized I was ready for a new challenge. The thrill of meeting daily sales targets and managing inventory had grown routine, so I decided it was time to pursue my passion for technology. Currently, I'm in the process of obtaining a Bachelor's degree from Western Governors University. Alongside my studies, I'm earning a suite of certifications including CompTIA A+, Security+, Network+, Project+, AWS Cloud Practitioner, and Linux Essentials.

But my journey doesn’t stop there! I’m also self-learning full-stack development, guided by the roadmap curated by a professional developer and content creator that goes by Smoljames. Balancing academic pursuits with practical coding projects, I’m eager to merge my newfound technical skills with real-world applications. This transition from retail to tech isn't just about career growth; it's about rekindling my excitement for a dynamic and ever-evolving field.

Outside of my studies and coding adventures, you’ll find me indulging in my love for anime, diving into classic fantasy novels, or getting lost in video games. Supporting me on this journey are four charming cats and my wonderful wife. Each day is a blend of learning, passion, and the pursuit of being better than I was yesterday.
